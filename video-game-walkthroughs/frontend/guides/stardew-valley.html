Tips for Beginners

  • Start by focusing on farming and gathering resources.
  • Build relationships with the townsfolk to unlock new content.
  • Plan your farm layout for optimal efficiency.

Advanced Strategies

  • Utilize the greenhouse for year-round farming.
  • Invest in artisan goods for higher profits.
  • Explore the mines for valuable resources and combat experience.
