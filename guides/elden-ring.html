Tips for Beginners

  • Don't rush the main storyline - explore thoroughly to find valuable gear and level up.
  • Level Vigor early to increase your survival chances against tough enemies.
  • Experiment with different weapons to find the moveset that suits your playstyle.
  • Use stealth mechanics to avoid unnecessary fights when exploring new areas.
  • Don't hesitate to use Spirit Ash summons for difficult boss encounters.
  • Upgrade your weapons before focusing too much on leveling attributes.
  • Read item descriptions to understand the rich lore and discover hidden secrets.

Advanced Strategies

  • Master the art of parrying for devastating critical hits against many tough enemies.
  • Create specialized builds focusing on either Intelligence, Faith, or Arcane for powerful late-game potential.
  • Use status effects like Bleed, Frost, and Scarlet Rot strategically against bosses with high HP pools.
  • Learn boss attack patterns and recognize windups to time your dodges perfectly.
  • Stack complementary talismans that enhance your specific build and playstyle.
  • Create specialized equipment loadouts for different situations (PvP, boss fights, exploration).
  • Use Torrent effectively in combat against large open-field bosses for mobility advantage.
